About Me

I am a person characterized by my relentless determination and unwavering resilience. I thrive on being outgoing and have an insatiable curiosity, especially when it comes to understanding how technology works. I adore meeting and interacting with people, which fuels my passion for connecting with the world. I was born in a small town, which created a deep affection for the simplicity of country life, particularly my love for farm animals, with horses and cows holding a special place in my heart. The most precious aspects of my life are my family, with a profound love for my mother and an unbreakable bond with my daughter. Cooking alongside my mother is a cherished activity that not only deepens my culinary skills but also serves as a meaningful connection to the recipes and memories passed down by my late grandmother. These two remarkable women have played an integral role in shaping the person I am today, and I hold them to the highest standard as my greatest sources of inspiration and strength. My blend of determination, curiosity, and a strong appreciation for family and tradition defines who I am as a person.
